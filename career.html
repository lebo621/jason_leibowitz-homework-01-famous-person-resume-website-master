Radiohead

Highlights of Radiohead's Career:

  • We caught our big break with the song Creep on our first album: Pablo Honey.
  • Our second album, The Bends, was fantastic rock n' roll.
  • Album three, OK Computer, was ground-breaking and revolutionary.
  • It's now the year 2000, we all survived Y2K and it was time to change the sound of rock by introducing computers into production. Enter our next album, Kid A. Our band's sound was beginning to navigate into unchartered waters. You be the judge: Everything In Its Right Place.
  • One year later we released our fifth album, Amnesiac. More computers, more cool sounds.
  • Hail to the Thief was our next album and had unique, weird but amazing songs. Check out the music video for There, There.
  • We changed the rules of the record industry when we released our next album, In Rainbows, online, without a label, and for free. F' the system!
  • The King of Limbs was album number eight in 2011. I like this one very much.
  • A Moon Shaped Pool, our latest album, was released on May 8, 2016. It is a work of art. Feel free to click on the album below to purchase it. Enjoy!
