Hello

I'm Thom Yorke. I am the lead singer of the band Radiohead. We have just released our 9th studio album, titled A Moon Shaped Pool, and I am quite proud of it. We are currently going on tour, so if you live on planet Earth, feel free to come listen to us play music.

A Bit of Background About My Life

I grew up in Northamptonshire, UK. I started playing guitar when I was 7 years old, and at 11 I wrote my first song and joined a band. I met my current bandmates when I was a teen at school, and our original band name was On A Friday. Several years later, in 1991, we signed onto a record label and changed our band name to Radiohead.
